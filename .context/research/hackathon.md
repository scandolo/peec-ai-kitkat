## Competition Rules & Submission Guidelines

### Submission Requirements

To qualify for the final judging, you must:

- **Submit your project by Sunday at 14:00**
- **Be a team of max. 5 people**
- **Use min. 3 partner technologies** (listed under resources)
- **Have created your project newly at this hackathon** (boilerplates are allowed)

### What needs to be submitted

**Project Presentation**
- Record a **2-minute video demo** of your project (using Loom or equivalent)
- Must include:
  - Detailed explanation of your solution
  - Demonstration of key features with a live walkthrough

**Open Source Repository**
- Provide a **public GitHub repository** containing your project's source code
- Must include:
  - Comprehensive **README** with setup and installation instructions
  - Clear documentation of all APIs, frameworks, and tools utilized
  - Sufficient technical documentation to enable thorough jury evaluation

## Competition Mode

Two-stage competition format, culminating in a live final presentation.

### Stage 1: Pre-Selection

- Build anything aligned with your creative vision — complete freedom of topic choice
- **8 finalist teams (1x per track)** advance to the Finalist Stage
- Judging criteria: creativity, technical complexity, with bonus points for effective use of partner technologies

### Stage 2: Finalist Stage

- All finalists showcase their projects **live before the jury and audience**
- Each team delivers a **5-minute presentation**
- Jury selects the **top 3 winners**

## Tracks

### Buena - The Context Engine
**Prize: 2,500 EUR**

Build an engine that produces a single Context Markdown File per property — a living, self-updating document containing every fact an AI agent needs to act. Dense, structured, traced to its source, surgically updated without destroying human edits.

### Qontext - Turn fragmented company data into a context base AI can operate on
**Prize: 1g real gold bar (1x per member) + private dinner with Qontext**

Turn a simulated enterprise dataset (email, CRM, HR, policy docs, etc.) into a structured company memory: a virtual file system plus graph that makes the company legible to both machines and humans.

### Inca - The Human Test
**Prize: AirPod Pros (1x per member)**

Build a phone-based voice agent that handles an inbound claim call and convinces the caller they are speaking to a human. Jury are the callers — highest human-pass rate wins.

### Hera - AI Agents for Video Generation
**Prize: AirPod Pros (1x per member)**

Build a creative agent that generates video or images to solve a specific problem. The agent should make editorial decisions on its own, guided by beliefs, presets, and tastes you give it.

### Peec AI - 0 -> 1 AI Marketer (OUR TRACK)
**Prize: 2,500 EUR**

Use the Peec AI MCP to build something that helps an early-stage brand win distribution against bigger competitors. Docs: https://docs.peec.ai/mcp/introduction

### Reonic - AI Renewable Designer
**Prize: 2,501 EUR, Reonic care package, team dinner with a founder and team leads**

Build an AI-powered solution that generates renewable energy system designs for residential customers.

### telli & ai-coustics - Voice AI that works in the wild
**Prize: Bose QuietComfort Ultra Headphones**

Build a real-time voice interface that holds up in the real world. Use the ai-coustics SDK to handle the audio layer. LiveKit quickstart plugin: https://github.com/livekit/plugins-ai-coustics-python

### Wildcard
**Prize: Qualification for the Finalist Stage (1x)**

Build whatever you want.
